Admission Process

📝
Step 1
Account Registration

Create an account on our portal to start the application process.

📤
Step 2
Form Responses & Document Submission

Fill out the application form and upload necessary documents for review.

💳
Step 3
Payment of Admission Fee

Complete the admission fee payment and schedule your placement test.

🗓️
Step 4
Screening/Placement Test

Attend the scheduled screening test to assess your skills and readiness

📬
Step 5
Application Status

Check your application status online for updates and further instructions. Note: Special learning needs assessment is available upon request.

📎
Step 6
Enrollment

Proceed to Registrar's office
