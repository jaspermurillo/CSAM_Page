Colegio San Agustin Makati is a leading Catholic school that nurtures students in the Augustinian tradition. With a vibrant mix of local and international learners, CSA fosters global citizenship, friendship, and personal growth. Beyond academics, students thrive through dynamic programs in sports and the arts—living true to its motto: Virtue and Science.

Early Childhood
Education
The EARLY CHILDHOOD EDUCATION (ECE) unit of Colegio San Agustin Makati welcomes pupils from 2.5 to 5 years old. Our programs, namely: Nursery, Junior Kinder, and Senior Kinder, provide pupils with developmentally appropriate activities that encourage them to explore, create, collaborate, and at the same time, have fun, and make friends.
Grade School
The Grade School Program is designed to provide the learners with a solid and well-rounded Christian doctrinal foundation and formation. The program also aims to lay the foundation for the Augustinians to become true servant leaders, the instruments of salvation for others and dedicated builders of the City of God.
Junior High School
The Junior High School (JHS) Program of CSA Makati is a PAASCU level III accredited program for Grades 7-10. It equips Augustinian learners with competencies necessary for subsequent learning leading to Senior High School and form them into Catholic servant-leaders in the tradition of Augustinian education.
Senior High School
The Senior High School (SHS) Program of CSA Makati develops learners in character and academic excellence. Our SHS graduates are well-rounded Catholic servant-leaders who are college-ready and future-proof. We offer the Academic Track that creates a pathway towards higher education. Learners explore possible college courses and future careers through various strands.
