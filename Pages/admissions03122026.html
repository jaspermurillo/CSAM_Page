Admission Guidelines & Policies

Our admission guidelines and policies for A.Y. 2026–2027 — everything you need to know before applying.

🎂

1. AGE REQUIREMENT (DepEd Order No. 015, s. 2025)

Senior Kinder: 5 years old on or before October 31. Children turning 5 between Nov. 1–Dec. 31 may still be admitted under certain conditions.

Grade 1: 6 years old on or before October 31. Must have completed Kindergarten with LRN from a DepEd-accredited school.

📊

2. GRADE REQUIREMENTS

General Average (GPA) of at least 85%
Conduct grade not lower than B (or equivalent)
STEM (Grade 11): at least 82% final grade in both Science 10 and Math 10.

💡

3. STUDENTS WITH SPECIAL LEARNING NEEDS

May be accommodated under certain conditions as determined by the Wellness Office.

📋

4. APPLICATION AND SCREENING PROCESS

All applicants must undergo the full application and screening process without exception.

🗂️

5. DOCUMENT POLICY

All credentials and documents submitted become the property of the CSA Makati Office of Admissions.

✅

6. AUTHENTICITY OF DOCUMENTS

Parents and students must ensure all documents submitted are true and correct.

⏱️

7. PROCESSING POLICY

Processing is on a first-come, first-served basis.

✉️

8. COMMUNICATION PROTOCOL

Only parents or legal guardians may send official communications. Representatives must present an authorization letter.

🔒

9. DATA PRIVACY AND CONSENT

By submitting an application, parents and guardians consent to the processing of personal, scholastic, and medical information for evaluation purposes.

📊

10. CONFIDENTIALITY OF TEST RESULTS

Admission test results are confidential and form part of internal evaluation tools.

🏫

11. SCHOOL RIGHT TO VERIFY

The school may conduct inquiries with the applicant's current school or attending physician. All gathered information is treated with strict confidentiality.

🤝

12. DECLARATION OF COMMITMENT

Completing the admissions process signifies the applicant's and parent's willingness to abide by all CSA rules and regulations.

🎉
