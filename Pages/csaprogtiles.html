👶 Early Childhood Education (ECE)
The EARLY CHILDHOOD EDUCATION (ECE) unit of Colegio San Agustin Makati welcomes pupils from 2.5 to 5 years old. Our programs—Nursery, Junior Kinder, and Senior Kinder—provide developmentally appropriate activities that encourage exploration, creativity, collaboration, and fun.
📚 Grade School
The Grade School Program is designed to provide the learners with a solid and well-rounded Christian doctrinal foundation and formation. The program also aims to lay the foundation for the Augustinians to become true servant leaders, instruments of salvation for others, and dedicated builders of the City of God.
🎒 Junior High School
The Junior High School (JHS) Program of CSA Makati is a PAASCU Level III accredited program for Grades 7 to 10. It equips Augustinian learners with competencies necessary for subsequent learning leading to Senior High School and forms them into Catholic servant-leaders in the tradition of Augustinian education.
🏫 Senior High School (SHS)
The SHS Program develops learners in character and academic excellence. Graduates are well-rounded Catholic servant-leaders who are college-ready and future-proof. The Academic Track offers pathways toward higher education and future careers.
